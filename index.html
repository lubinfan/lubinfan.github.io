About

I am currently a Staff Algorithm Engineer at Apsaras Lab, Alibaba Cloud. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Peter Wonka at the Visual Computing Center, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), from 2014 to 2017. I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University in 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Liu.

My research interests lies at the intersection of computer graphics, computer vision, and vision-language models.
